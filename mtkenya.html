Mt Kenya Region Leadership

The Mt Kenya Region of Jesus Healing Sanctuary Ministries (JHSM) is a vibrant and growing area under dedicated leadership of Bsh Simon Lemarimbe. Our regional overseer, together with a team of committed pastors, has faithfully overseen the spiritual growth, discipleship, and outreach efforts across the region's churches.

Other Men of God under Bishop Simon Lemarimbe are: Reverend Jacob Macharia who is the lead pastor, Pastor Peter Gichuru, Pastor John Wanjohi and Evangelist Naftaly Machangi.

Their ministry reflects a deep commitment to nurturing believers, empowering leaders, and extending the love of Christ throughout the Mt Kenya area, including their neighbouring communities.

Our Church in Mt Kenya Region

JHSM Nyeri

Regional Headquarters

JHSM Ikonge serves as the headquarters of the Nyanza region, strategically located in the heart of Ikonge town. The church has become a spiritual beacon in the community, drawing people from surrounding areas to experience God's love and transformation.

Under dedicated pastoral leadership, the church has grown significantly, with vibrant ministries for children, youth, and adults. The congregation is known for its passionate worship and commitment to community outreach.

Through the tireless leadership of our regional overseer, the Nyanza Region of JHSM stands as a testimony of faith, excellence, and spiritual impact. Each church under this oversight contributes uniquely to the mission of spreading the Gospel and transforming lives across the region.
