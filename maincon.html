About Impartation Conference

The Impartation Conference is a week long transformative gathering that happens Annually in August/September in our mother church JHSM Maralal, Samburu. It designed to equip believers with spiritual tools and insights needed to walk in their divine purpose. Through powerful teachings, prayer sessions, and community fellowship, participants experience personal growth and spiritual renewal.

"In an era marked by rapid spiritual, social, and technological transformation, the Impartation Conference seeks to equip believers with divine wisdom, unwavering values, and spiritual resilience; raising a generation of leaders empowered to influence their world through the power of the Holy Spirit."

Why Impartation Conference?

How can believers be equipped and empowered through the Word and the Spirit to walk in divine purpose, power, and transformation?

In today's challenging world, believers need more than just Sunday sermons. They need practical tools, deep spiritual encounters, and a supportive community to help them navigate life's complexities while staying true to their divine calling.
