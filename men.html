Who We Are & Our Mission

Men of Zion is a brotherhood of men committed to walking boldly in the truth of God's Word. We are men of faith who stand firm in purpose, lead with integrity, and sharpen one another in spirit and character.

Rooted in scripture and built on accountability, this fellowship exists to raise men who are pillars in their homes, beacons in their workplaces, and warriors in the Kingdom of God. We believe that a man transformed by Christ transforms everything around him.

Fellowship Programs

Men's Bible Study

Weekly deep-dive sessions into Scripture designed to challenge and build every man's foundation in the Word.

Prayer Camps

Quarterly overnight prayer retreats where men intercede for families, the church, and the nation.

Mentorship Circles

Structured peer-accountability groups pairing younger men with seasoned brothers walking the same road of faith.

Men's Conference

An annual gathering of men for teaching, worship, and strategic conversations about life, family, and Kingdom purpose.
