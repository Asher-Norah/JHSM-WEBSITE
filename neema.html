Neema Women's Fellowship

Grace. Strength. Purpose in Christ.

Our Story

Neema, meaning "Grace" in Swahili, is a movement inspiring women to rise in faith, embrace their God-given purpose, and support one another in spiritual growth. Our conference brings together women from all walks of life to experience transformation through worship, teaching, and fellowship.

Founded on the principles of divine grace and sisterhood, Ne'ema has become a beacon of hope and empowerment for women seeking to deepen their relationship with Christ and impact their communities.

"Empowering women to walk boldly in grace."
