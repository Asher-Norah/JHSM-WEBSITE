WELCOME TO

JESUS HEALING SANCTUARY MINISTRIES

At Jesus Healing Sanctuary Ministries, our vision is to create a dwelling place for God’s presence a sanctuary where hearts are healed, souls are restored, and lives are transformed by the power of Jesus Christ. We are a ministry rooted in faith, love, and the Word, called to reach the nations with the message of hope, deliverance, and renewal.

We believe that true healing begins in His presence. That is why we remain devoted to cultivating an atmosphere where the Holy Spirit moves freely, bringing revival to hearts and strength to the weary. At JHSM, you are not just a part of a ministry you are part of a family walking in divine purpose, empowered to bring light to the world.

With hearts of thanksgiving, we honour every partner, intercessor, and friend who stands with us in faith. Your prayers, love, and support fuel the vision God has entrusted to us. May the peace of Jesus be your portion, His joy your song, and His healing touch forever rest upon you.
