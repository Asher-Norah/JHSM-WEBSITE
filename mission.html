Our Mission & Beliefs

The foundation of our faith and the principles that guide our ministry

Mission Statement

Restoring hope and dignity to the poor, the marginalized, and the broken, through acts of compassion, spiritual mentorship, and community empowerment.

Vision Statement

A caring and inclusive society where Jesus is worshiped while dignity and needs of every person are honored and restored.

This vision reflects the church's commitment to being a beacon of hope and spiritual healing within the community, nurturing a future where all, especially the vulnerable, are embraced with Christlike love.

Doctrinal Statement

At JHSM, we believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God — the foundation of all truth and practice. We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

Salvation is by grace through faith in Him alone, and through His redeeming blood, all who believe receive forgiveness, new life, and the promise of eternal glory. We believe in the ministry and power of the Holy Spirit, who dwells in believers, enabling holy living, spiritual growth, and service. The Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, disciple, and reach the world with the Gospel.

Ministry Philosophy

We believe that transformation begins with the heart. Our philosophy is to lead people from spiritual lostness to fully committed, Christ-centered labourers.

Through a balanced focus on evangelism and spiritual growth, we aim to fulfill the Great Commission in our generation—raising lives deeply rooted in Christ and ready to serve with passion and purpose.

Statement of Faith

At JHSM, the Word of God stands at the center of all we do. We believe the Bible comprising the 66 books of the Old and New Testaments is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.

We believe in the Holy Trinity that Godhead consists of God the Father, God the Son and the Holy Spirit; Three in One and One in Three.

We believe the Bible to be the inspired authoritative Word of God (2 Peter 1:21).

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His incarnation, crucifixion, divine bodily resurrection, His ascension to glory and His promised second coming.

We believe in salvation through the redeeming blood of Jesus Christ, which is by grace through faith and not by works.

We believe in Jesus' power to save, to heal and sustain Christians to live holy and pure lives by the enabling of the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the observance of water baptism after salvation; immersion in the name of God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit (Mt 28:19–20) and the Lord's supper as commended in the Holy Bible.

We believe marriage is honorable it should be between two adults, male and female who agree to be joined together as husband and wife. We believe a holy matrimony is between two born again believers of opposite sex who publicly come and take their vows before the church to live together as husband and wife in accordance to the word of God (2 Cor. 6:14–18.

We believe marriage was intended to be a life covenant between the couple; after death the one who remains is free to re-marry; divorce is not acceptable in our society except as commended in the word of God. (Mal. 2; 14–16; 1 Cor. 7:10–15; Dt. 24:1; Mat. 19:4–9).

We believe in the bodily resurrection, eternal life for the righteous and eternal punishment for the wicked.

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Ghost with manifestation of speaking in tongues (Acts 2:4, 10:46, 19:6) and the present ministry of the Spirit in and through the believers as manifested in the Ministry gifts and fruit of the Holy Spirit. (Eph. 4:11, 1 Cor. 12:9–11, Gal. 5:22–23

Standards of Ministry

Living and Serving Together in the Spirit of Christ

At JHSM, we believe ministry flows from family. Whether you're a leader, volunteer, staff member, or part of our global community, we commit to walking together in unity, love, and maturity.

We honour each other with trust and grace: We assume the best, speak well of one another, and choose love even in uncertainty.

We handle conflict the Jesus way: We address issues directly, kindly, and biblically choosing reconciliation over rumour.

We speak truth in love: Even in tough conversations, we lead with gentleness and seek growth—not harm.

We serve as one body with one heart: We're in this together. Every role matters. Every person counts.

We show up for God and each other: We stand with one another—in prayer, in presence, and in encouragement.

At the core of it all is love. A love that reflects Jesus. A love that defines our culture. A love that makes room for everyone to belong, grow, and thrive.

Our Commitments

The Culture We Carry. The Legacy We Build.

At JHSM, we live out our vision by the way we lead, serve, and relate. Our commitments reflect who we are becoming and the lasting impact we hope to leave:

lead by example: We don't just teach the Word—we live it, every day, in every space.

We take full responsibility:Words matter. Actions matter. We own our influence and strive to honour Christ in all things.

We treat everyone with dignity:Every person is made in God's image. That truth defines how we interact with respect, kindness, and honour.

We grow people, not platforms: Ministry is people. We're here to disciple, empower, and walk alongside others into their God-given calling.

We grow ourselves to better serve: Through continuous trainings, humility, feedback, and prayer, we commit to becoming better because growth is a form of worship.

We celebrate diversity and walk in unity: We cherish our different backgrounds and gifts yet move forward with one purpose: Jesus.

We live with legacy in mind: We serve with eternity in view—planting seeds of hope and truth that will bless generations.

At the center of it all is Jesus. His Gospel is our message. His Spirit is our guide. His love is our heartbeat. We believe He's still changing lives and we're honoured to be a part of it.
